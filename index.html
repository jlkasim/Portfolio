About Me

Hi, my name is James. I am a multimedia student at FEU Tech, currently in my 2nd year. I’ve been passionate about digital art since 2020, and over time I’ve worked as a logo and background artist for major Roblox games. I love creating digital art and exploring photography.

My artwork commissions use Photoshop and Clip Studio Paint. I create headshots, half body/group art, and full body illustrations. For photography, I cover events, portraits, and street/environment shots. Prices are negotiable depending on your budget.
